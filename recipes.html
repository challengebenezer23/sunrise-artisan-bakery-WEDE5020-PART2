Classic French Toast

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Butter for frying
  • Maple syrup, for serving
Instructions:
  1. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices into the mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Fry bread slices for 2â€“3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with maple syrup.

Hearty Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices multigrain bread
  • 2 slices turkey or ham
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise or mustard
Instructions:
  1. Spread mayonnaise or mustard on bread slices.
  2. Layer lettuce, tomato, cheese, and meat on one slice.
  3. Top with the second slice of bread.
  4. Cut in half and serve fresh.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices white or sourdough bread
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese (or cheese of choice)
  • Butter, softened
Instructions:
  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of bread.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place sandwich buttered side down.
  4. Cook until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Slice and serve hot.

Classic Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices white or wheat bread
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp fruit jelly or jam
Instructions:
  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  3. Press together, slice in half, and enjoy!
