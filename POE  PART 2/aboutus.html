About Sunrise Artisan Bakery

Our Story

Sunrise Artisan Bakery was founded to bring wholesome, handmade bread and pastries to Cape Town’s community. We value sustainable practices and local engagement.

Our Values

  • Quality Ingredients – locally sourced and organic
  • Handmade Craftsmanship – careful hand-shaping and baking
  • Sustainable – zero food waste, green energy, compostable packaging
  • Community – local charities and support for Cape Town initiatives

Mission & Vision

Mission: Bringing wholesome, handmade baked goods to our community through sustainable local sourcing.
Vision: To be Cape Town’s trusted, quality-driven community bakery.
