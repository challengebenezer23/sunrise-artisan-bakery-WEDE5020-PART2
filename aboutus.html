About Us

Our Story

Sunrise Artisan Bakery was founded with a simple idea: to bring the warmth of home-baked bread and pastries to Cape Town’s vibrant community. Our journey began with a passion for wholesome, handmade goods and a commitment to sustainable practices.

Our Values

  • Quality Ingredients
  • Handmade Craftsmanship
  • Sustainable Practices
  • Community Engagement

Mission & Vision

Mission Statement:
To bring wholesome, handmade baked goods to the community using sustainable, locally sourced ingredients.

Vision Statement:
To be Cape Town’s leading artisan bakery, known for quality, sustainability, and community involvement.
